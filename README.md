# InterCooperative Network (ICN)

Empowering cooperatives globally through digital collaboration and resource sharing, the ICN is an open-source platform designed for and by the cooperative community. Aimed at harnessing the collective strength of cooperatives, the ICN facilitates innovation, advocacy, and shared prosperity in a digital landscape.

## Vision

To build an interconnected ecosystem that empowers cooperatives with tools for innovation, advocacy, and growth, ensuring a cooperative-led future that values democracy, equity, and mutual aid.

## Core Features

The ICN integrates various features to support its mission:

- **Democratic Governance and Membership System:** Ensuring that all cooperative members have an equal say in the platform's evolution, embodying the one-member-one-vote principle.
  
- **Identity Provider (IdP) and Federated Identity Management (FIM):** Streamlining access across the ICN with secure authentication and single sign-on capabilities.
  
- **Cooperative Marketplace and B2B Exchange:** Facilitating economic opportunities through a platform for cooperatives to sell products, services, and engage in joint procurement.
  
- **Communication and Collaboration Tools:** Offering a suite of tools for real-time communication, project management, and knowledge sharing among cooperatives.
  
- **Data Exchange and Analytics:** Providing a secure mechanism for cooperatives to share and analyze data, supporting informed decision-making and strategic planning.
  
- **Education and Training Portal:** Delivering a comprehensive array of educational resources focused on cooperative development, digital skills, and more.
  
- **Advocacy Platform:** Amplifying the collective voice of the cooperative movement on policy and social issues.
  
- **Voting and Decision-Making System:** A digital platform for secure, transparent voting on key network matters and governance.

## Contributing

The ICN thrives on community contributions. While we are in the early stages and primarily focused on planning and design, there are many ways to contribute:

- **Feedback and Ideas:** Share your insights, suggestions, and innovative ideas to shape the ICN.
- **Documentation and Resources:** Help create valuable resources, guides, and documentation for current and future participants.
- **Advocacy and Outreach:** Assist in spreading the word about the ICN, engaging cooperatives, and building our community.
